Stability Analysis

Comprehensive solutions for analyzing the stability of earth slopes, rock slopes, and man-made structures using both analytical methods and FEM.

Stability of Earth Slopes

Determine the worst circular or polygonal slip surface and solve specific groundwater scenarios whilst incorporating stabilization elements such as anchors, nails, reinforcements, and piles.

Included Programs: Slope Stability Anti-Slide Pile

Stability of Rock Slopes

Solve the stability of rock slopes according to planar or polygonal slip surfaces. Includes advanced analysis of rock wedges using the stereographic Lambert projection.

Included Programs: Rock Stability

Man-Made Slopes

Design slopes reinforced with geotextiles, nails, shotcrete, or steel meshing. Perform verifications for internal stability, slip on reinforcement, overturning, and bearing capacity.

Included Programs: MSE Wall Nailed Slopes

Advanced FEM Analysis

Analyze groundwater using numerical seepage calculations for steady and transient flow. Model generic slopes using stepwise reduction of shear strength parameters.

Included Programs: FEM FEM Water Flow
