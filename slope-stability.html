Comprehensive Analytical Methods

Analyze circular and polygonal slip surfaces using all general methods, optimized for fast and reliable results.

  • Methods: Bishop, Fellenius/Petterson, Spencer, Morgenstern-Price, Sarma, Janbu, Shahunyanc.
  • Material Models: According to Mohr-Coulomb and Hoek-Brown.
  • Analysis in both effective and total parameters of soils.
  • Fast and reliable optimization of slip surfaces.
  • Handles foliation of soils and tensile cracks.

Advanced Modeling Capabilities

Model complex geotechnical situations effortlessly with built-in catalogs and dynamic environmental factors.

  • Presence of water modeled through ground water tables or pore pressure isolines.
  • Rapid draw-down analysis capabilities.
  • Arbitrary number of surcharges (strip, trapezoidal, concentrated).
  • Arbitrary number of anchors and geo-reinforcements.
  • Built-in database of soils, rocks, and manufacturer catalogs for anchors.
  • Earthquake effects and simple modeling of rigid bodies.

Seamless Workflow & BIM Support

GEO5 Slope Stability doesn't work in isolation. It communicates with your entire drafting and engineering ecosystem.

  • Handles successive construction of slopes within one single analysis task.
  • DWG and DXF import/export for direct CAD communication.
  • BIM-compatible export via the IFC interface.
  • Integrates directly with Slope Stability - Water Flow for groundwater seepage analysis.
