TRUSS4

TRUSS4 Roofing Software

The complete solution for timber roof trusses connected with punched metal plate fasteners—from 3D modeling directly to manufacturing.

Core Applications

Truss 3D

Comprehensive 3D modeling of complex roof structures, including automatic hip and valley generation.

  • Roof Generator
  • 3D Visualization

Truss 2D

Detailed structural analysis of individual trusses, checking timber elements and metal plate capacities.

  • Statics Analysis
  • Joint Verification

Truss Explorer

Centralized project management tool for organizing files, generating batch reports, and tracking jobs.

  • Project Management
  • Batch Processing

The Complete Workflow

A seamless pipeline from initial architectural concept to factory production.

1

Modeling

Begin by defining the roof's geometry in Truss 3D. The software automatically handles intersections, hips, valleys, and generates the necessary truss layouts based on standard structural rules.

2

Structural Analysis

Individual elements are transferred to Truss 2D. Here, internal forces are calculated, and timber cross-sections and punched metal plate fasteners are rigorously verified according to chosen regional design codes.

3

Documentation

Automatically generate comprehensive outputs including static calculation reports, material take-offs (timber volume and plate quantities), and precise 2D production drawings.

4

Manufacturing BTL / BVX / Hundegger

Export the finalized project directly to factory hardware. TRUSS4 supports native exports to automated CNC timber cutting saws and laser-guided laser projection systems for plate assembly.
